Caffe bar

Caffettiera (moka coffee pot in italian) is a 90’s inspired Italian bar located in Montreal’s Golden Square Mile. For Italians, the “bar” is where one goes to take a break from the day.Our espresso is roasted in Italy and brewed from shiny Vittorio Arduini Lever machines as well as classic Bialettis.

About Us

Featuring historic building details dating back to 1875, The Green Room showcases high to a ceilings, exposed brick, one of a kind artwork commemorating old and new Nashville, and more. Our Executive Chef and his team create mouth watering dishes that will have your'guests talking about your event for years to come.

Nashville’s Green Room is Nashville’s premier event space with lounge seating, exposed too brick, one of a kind artwork and décor, and sound capabilities for any function. This 5,000 square foot lounge bar offers both 2nd Avenue and riverfront views to create a great experience for your event.
